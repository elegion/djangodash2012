Random

Generates random string. First param: string length. Second param: any combination of d (digits), l (ascii lower), L (ascii upper) and any other symbols.

Examples:

{random}@touchin.ru → D13FA@touchin.ru

{random:3:d} → 458

{random:4:dLl_} → 1B_a
